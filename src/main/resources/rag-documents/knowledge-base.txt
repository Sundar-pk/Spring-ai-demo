== COMPANY AI PLATFORM KNOWLEDGE BASE ==

SECTION 1: APPROVED AI TOOLS
Our company has approved the following AI tools for internal use:
- InternalLLM v2.0: Our self-hosted large language model running on the AI Platform cluster.
  Endpoint: http://ai-platform.internal/v1 | Auth: Bearer token from the IAM portal.
- CodeAssist Pro: AI code completion tool approved for all engineering teams.
- DataInsight AI: Approved for analytics and BI teams to query data using natural language.
- Anything NOT on this list requires a security review before use.

SECTION 2: DATA CLASSIFICATION RULES
When using AI tools you MUST follow these data handling rules:
- PUBLIC data (green): Can be sent to any approved AI tool without restrictions.
- INTERNAL data (yellow): Can only be sent to InternalLLM. Never to external services.
- CONFIDENTIAL data (red): Must NOT be sent to any AI tool without CISO approval.
- Customer PII (names, emails, IDs) is always CONFIDENTIAL.

SECTION 3: LLM MODEL SPECIFICATIONS
InternalLLM v2.0 specifications:
- Context window: 128,000 tokens
- Supported languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Japanese, Chinese
- Fine-tuned on: Company documentation, internal wikis, code repositories
- Max output tokens: 4,096
- Rate limits: 100 requests/minute per team, 1,000 requests/minute enterprise-wide

SECTION 4: INCIDENT RESPONSE
If you observe unexpected or harmful AI output:
1. Stop using the tool immediately.
2. Document the exact prompt and response.
3. Report to ai-safety@company.internal within 24 hours.
4. Do NOT share harmful outputs externally or on social media.
SLA: All AI incidents are reviewed within 48 hours by the AI Safety team.

SECTION 5: HOW TO REQUEST ACCESS
To get access to the AI Platform:
1. Submit an access request at https://iam.internal/request
2. Your manager must approve the request.
3. Complete the mandatory "Responsible AI Use" training (2 hours, on the LMS).
4. Access is provisioned within 3 business days after approval + training.

SECTION 6: BILLING AND COST
AI Platform usage is billed to team cost centers:
- Token-based pricing: $0.002 per 1K tokens (input + output).
- Monthly budget alerts are sent at 80% and 100% of the team's allocated budget.
- Budget increases require approval from your VP.
- Cost dashboards are available at https://ai-costs.internal
